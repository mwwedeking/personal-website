ABOUT

Hello, my name is Miles Wedeking. I am 25 years old and I'm a current senior at the University of South Carolina, studying Computer Information Systems. I was born and raised on Hilton Head Island, South Carolina. I have developed a deep passion for learning about the world as well as striving to become the best version of myself! You have reached my personal website, which I have used to practice front-end web development skills and show case some of my personal and professional achievements.

My Mother and Father raised me in such a way as to always embrace my spontaneous and abstract thinking. Early on, I developed an interest in how things work. I began to question observations that I made about life, beyond the scope of what meets the eye. Being a teen who struggled to sit still, I managed to express myself through my passions in antique vehicle mechanics, computers, and athletics.

In my senior year of high school, I answered the call to defend my nation by enlisting in the United States Marine Corps in September, 2017. Having no idea the impact that this would have on my character development, I committed to making the most of every opportunity I had. It was during these four years that I truly discovered how important it is for me to forever strive to becoming the best version of myself possible.
